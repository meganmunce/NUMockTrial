What is college mock trial?

College mock trial is a competitive public speaking ability governed by the American Mock Trial Association. Students compete as both attorneys and witnesses, giving speeches, acting as characters, and engaging in impromptu objection battles.

We compete against other colleges across the country in front of real lawyers and judges using rules based on the federal rules of evidence. Each year, we try a new case. This year, the case is about XXX. To learn more about mock trial and this year's case, come to an info session!
